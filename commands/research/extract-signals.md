# Extract Signals

## Purpose
Turn raw notes or articles into signals (patterns) and prompts (angles) to guide outlines.

## Usage
Paste text and ask: “Extract patterns and propose 3–5 prompts.”

## Notes
Favor cross-domain patterns; avoid restating headlines.
