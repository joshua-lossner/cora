# Procedure — Privacy Review

Purpose
- Ensure alignment with minimal data, clear consent, and user rights.

Steps
1) Identify data collected, purposes, retention, and processors.
2) Review consent copy and flows; propose reductions and clarifications.
3) Summarize risks and recommendations; link to policies.

Expected
- Short findings brief with actionable recommendations.
